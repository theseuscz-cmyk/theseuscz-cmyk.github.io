NeuronIdle Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026
NeuronIdle is a free idle game developed by NeuronIdle Team (Thendril).
Data Collection
NeuronIdle currently stores all game progress locally on your device. Future versions may introduce optional online features (leaderboards, brain marketplace, Google Play sign-in) which will require collection of:
Google account identifier (for authentication)
Game progress data (for online features)
Username/display name (for leaderboards)
These features will be opt-in and clearly communicated before any data is collected.
Advertisements
NeuronIdle uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifier, IP address (for general location), device information (model, OS version), and interactions with ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
In-App Purchases
NeuronIdle offers optional in-app purchases (Neuron Units — premium currency). Purchases are processed securely through Google Play. NeuronIdle does not store any payment or billing information. All transactions are handled by Google Play according to Google's Terms of Service.
In-app purchases are not required to enjoy the full game experience.
Third Party Services
NeuronIdle minimizes data collection. We do not use third-party analytics, behavioral tracking, or social media SDKs. The only integrated services are:
Google AdMob (advertising)
Google Play services (distribution, billing, and standard crash reporting handled by Google Play Console)
Google Play Games Services (future — authentication, leaderboards)
Data Storage
Online game data (when introduced in future versions) will be stored securely on servers in accordance with applicable data protection laws including GDPR.
Your Rights (GDPR)
If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data: access, correction, deletion, portability, and the right to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.
Children
NeuronIdle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us at the email below.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy as NeuronIdle evolves. Significant changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
